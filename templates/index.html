Check if your voicemail is vulnerable.

This tool only works if you have not transferred your number to another carrier (and your phone number and carrier are Australian).
If you have transferred your number, the only sure-fire way of checking if you're vulnerable is to call this number: 1800 80 1920. If you are told the carrier is "Optus", then you are vulnerable.
